Who I Am

Code. Create. Innovate.

I'm Prosper Ugwuegbu, a software developer and 200-level Computer Science student at FUTO. My journey into tech started when I was fascinated by how a few lines of code could bring something entirely new into existence — and that fascination has never faded.

I specialize in building full-stack web applications, crafting pixel-perfect interfaces, and architecting scalable backend systems. Every project I take on is an opportunity to push my limits and deliver something that genuinely makes a difference.

When I'm not deep in a codebase, I'm contributing to open-source projects, exploring emerging technologies, or mentoring fellow students breaking into tech. I believe in technology as a tool for empowerment — especially on the African continent.
